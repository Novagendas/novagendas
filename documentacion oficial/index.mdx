Novagendas is a web-based appointment management platform built for aesthetic clinics, spas, and health service businesses. It centralizes your entire operation — from scheduling and client records to payments and inventory — so your team can focus on delivering great service instead of managing paperwork.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your subdomain">
    Your business gets its own dedicated subdomain (e.g., `yourbusiness.novagendas.com`). Open it in any browser to reach your login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your credentials">
    Use the email and password provided by your administrator. Your role (Admin, Receptionist, or Specialist) determines which features you can access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your services and team">
    As an Admin, go to **Services** to define what you offer and **Team** to add your staff before scheduling appointments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule your first appointment">
    Open the **Agenda** and click any time slot to create an appointment. Assign a client, service, and specialist — done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Novagendas covers

Novagendas is designed as an internal operations tool for your staff — not a client self-booking portal. Your team manages appointments received through calls, WhatsApp, or walk-ins all in one place.
